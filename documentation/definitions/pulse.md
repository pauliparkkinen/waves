## Definition

Pulse is the current status of the patient in the hospital system's context. It should be updated, whenever there is any activity recorded to the patient relevant for the context of the organisation.

## Properties
- Pulse has an urgency. The urgency is calculated based on the scores of the activities recorded related to the patient. The pulse with the highest urgency should be reviewed first.
- Organisation id -- Pulse is connected to one hospital system or hospital
